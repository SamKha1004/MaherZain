About me

Maher Zain is a Lebanese-Swedish R&B singer, songwriter and music producer. He released his debut album Thank You Allah, an internationally successful album produced by Awakening Records, in 2009. He released his follow-up album Forgive Me in April 2012 under the same production company, and a third album One in 2016. Born: 16 July 1981 (age 41 years), Tripoli, Lebanon Spouse: Aicha Ameziane (m. 2009) Children: Abdullah Maher Zain, Yasmine Maher Zain, Aya Maher Zain Nationality: Lebanese, Swedish Parents: Mustafa Maher
